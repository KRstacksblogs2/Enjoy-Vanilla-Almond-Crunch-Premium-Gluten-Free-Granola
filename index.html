Product Information

We created Vanilla Almond Crunch to be the creamy, crunchy star of your pantry. Out of all our flavors, it was the very first we developed and for good reason. It balances crunchy oats, slivered almonds, organic honey, and pure ground vanilla bean to elevate everyday moments.

Designed by Chef Laura Briscoe, every batch is handcrafted and carefully baked to deliver the perfect trifecta of taste, texture, and mouthfeel. We believe granola should be more than just a breakfast food—it should be a delightful, anytime indulgence that feels both satisfying and nourishing.

With certified gluten-free oats as the foundation, we’ve ensured this granola is accessible to those with dietary sensitivities while never compromising on flavor or quality. It’s also non-GMO, kosher certified, and made without artificial additives or preservatives.

Whether you're enjoying it as a cereal, mixing it into baked goods, or sprinkling it over a smoothie bowl, Vanilla Almond Crunch brings elevated simplicity to every bite.
